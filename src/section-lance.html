Fibroid Treatment Options for women everywhere

Your Treatment Options

Treatment for fibroids varies based on symptom severity, fibroid size and location, reproductive considerations and available treatment expertise. Available treatments include:

MEDICATIONS

To manage symptoms, such as hormonal treatments that regulate menstrual cycles or, target hormones that regulate Fibroid growth.

Minimally invasive procedures

 This includes Uterine Artery Embolization (UAE), which kills Fibroids by cutting off their blood supply. Alternative procedure is Myolysis, which uses electric current or laser to destroy Fibroids and shrink blood vessels.

Surgical options

Myomectomy (removal of fibroids, preserving the uterus) or, Hysterectomy (removal of the uterus as a definitive solution that prevents pregnancies).

SHANISKA HANNA

Nassau, Bahamas

I just want to start by saying what a journey it has been!!  I am just so excited to be doing this testimonial!!  

So often we as women suffer in silence with so many female issues. I myself have suffered with Fibroids since the age of 23yrs old. I remember the day I received the doctor’s diagnosis, I felt so depressed and defeated!! She had advised that I should have hysterectomy at the age of 23yrs!!! Wow!!! Off course, I did not take that advice and decided to seek natural remedies. 

 

Over the years, my symptoms worsened, heavy bleeding, bloating, excessive pain and tiredness etc. Fast forward to the age of 32 yrs old, I had decided to have a myomectomy, after which I felt immediate relief. The doctor advised me that there were so many fibroids and he could not extract them all…he took out the larger ones that seemed to be causing my symptoms. My relief lasted for several years and the symptoms slowly returned.

The symptoms worsened. I would dread having my periods because they were so painfuI with vomiting and diarrhea. I would miss work at least once a month because of it. I remember just always wanting to be at home during that time because I was bound to have an accident. I would get up from my seat and realized that my clothes and seat were soiled in blood. I bled so heavily. I would use 3-4 extra large pads a day. My stomach was so large until people consistently thought I was pregnant.

 

Something had to be done. I was tired both physically and emotionally!! I began my search for a method that was minimally invasive and effective. I was led to Dr. Mikhail Higgins. I was so relieved and excited. We finally offered such a treatment here in Nassau (Bahamas), which was previously  only offered abroad.   

I mentioned it to my gynecologist I was seeing at the time, who advised me that it would not work for me because my Fibroids were too big and too many!! She advised me to have a  myomectomy (again!!) or a hysterectomy. Neither of these were an option for me!!  

I am so glad I am a woman of great faith!!! In spite  of my doctor’s advice and without a referral letter, with the support of my husband, I decided to make an appointment to see Dr. Higgins. I said to myself, let him be the one to tell me it could not be done, which he never said!! After our first meeting, I finally felt hopeful!! I was on my way to freedom!!  

I had the procedure done in 2023. Now in my forties, my life has totally changed!!! I am free!!!No more heavy bleeding!!! No more awful periods!! No more embarrassment!! I am so glad I didn’t take the advice of any of the doctors! I would encourage any woman to choose this option. God has the final say!!!  It was a YES for me!!

-Shaniska
