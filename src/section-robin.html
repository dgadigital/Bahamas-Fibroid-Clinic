Community and Wellness Engagement

A strong advocate for diversity and inclusion, Dr. Higgins cofounded the UPHS-CHOP Alliance of Minority Physicians. His community involvement extends to mentoring underrepresented students and sharing his expertise as a yoga instructor and performance coach for athletes, demonstrating a holistic approach to health and leadership.

Bringing New Medical Techniques to the Bahamas and Caribbean

Dr. Mikhail Higgins is the country's first board-certified fellowship-trained Interventional Radiologist. He is on the Family Medicine Center medical team. Through his collaboration with Family Medicine Center, Dr. Higgins brings innovative new medical techniques to the Bahamas and the Caribbean, previously unavailable in the country.
