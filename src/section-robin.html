MEET YOUR LEADING INTERVENTIONAL RADIOLOGIST

Dr.Mikhail Higgins

A Pioneer in Minimally Invasive Fibroid Treatment

Dr. Mikhail C.S.S. Higgins, the Bahamas’ first board-certified, fellowship-trained Interventional Radiologist, spearheads the Bahamas Fibroid & Interventional Clinic at Family Medicine Center. He founded a clinic specifically for educating and treating women suffering from fibroids, introducing the minimally invasive uterine artery embolization (UAE) as a better alternative to major surgery.

Educated at prestigious institutions like the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania and Yale University, Dr. Higgins is an expert in therapeutic minimally invasive interventions focusing on women’s health. He is renowned for his commitment to medical excellence, patient safety, healthcare education, anti-burnout strategies, and diversity initiatives. Beyond medicine, he’s a certified yoga instructor and high-performance coach, passionately advocating for women’s wellness optimization.
